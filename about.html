About - JFK Declassified Document Search

The following collections related to the JFK assassination have been declassified and released. The pages of each document have been OCR'd and full-text indexed for retrieval on this site. The source files are hosted by the National Archives. The OCR process is FAR from perfect, but it should help navigate the 278K-page archive.

  • July 24, 2017 (3,810 documents - complete)
  • October 26, 2017 (2,891 documents - complete)
  • November 3, 2017 (676 documents - complete)
  • November 9, 2017 (13,213 documents - complete)
  • November 17, 2017 (10,744 documents - complete)
  • December 15, 2017 (3,539 documents - complete)

For more information on the source documents, please visit the US National Archives.

I am not a historian or subject-matter expert on anything related to these files, but I thought it would make for a cool project to brush up on some skills. If you have any questions, please feel free to email me.






Legal Disclaimer: The information on the website is provided "as is". Inqueries should always refer to the source content in the National Archives.
