JFK Declassified Document Search

Search the fulltext archive of documents related to the assassination of John F. Kennedy, declassified in 2017.

These documents have been released in to the National Archives and we have OCR'd and fulltext indexed them to help navigating the 34K documents (approx. 278K pages).


Search tips: Use * for wildcard searches and quotes for an exact match (e.g. "tropicana casino").
